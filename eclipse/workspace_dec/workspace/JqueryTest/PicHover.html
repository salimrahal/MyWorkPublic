What people say about CannyBill

Why use hosted software?

The price of the software is included in the monthly fee. Setup is included and automated. PCI compliance affects all on site gateways and for all auto-bill transactions.

CannyBill order forms

CannyBill enables you send automated renewal invoices with automated payment reminder notifications and optional late fees. You can set when an invoice should be due and how many and often reminders should be sent out.

Security

We are dedicated to ensuring that you data is stored safely, securely and reliably. Our security safeguards give you peace of mind that your information is treated with the care and responsibility it requires.

Manage customers

Manage every aspect of your customers account. Generate new invoices or see how many are overdue or which ones have been partially paid. You can also re-send login details, credit accounts, upgrade or downgrade a package and update contact details.

Access for you and staff

CannyBill enables you to setup accounts for your staff members so they too can access CannyBill and provide support. You can also setup permissions for each login account.

Send powerful email campaigns

Signing up customers is one thing but keeping them is another. CannyBill enables to you to either post Latest News items in your customers account which they would see when they logged in and also send powerful email campaigns.
